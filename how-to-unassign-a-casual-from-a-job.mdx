title: "How to Unassign a Casual from a Job"
---

<Frame>
  ![2024 05 28 11 48 54 1024x495](/images/2024-05-28_11-48-54-1024x495.png)
</Frame>

1. Select Calendar
2. Select the Date of the Job
3. Select the Job
4. Click the X button next to the Casual you wish to unassign

<Frame>
  ![Image 14](/images/image-14.png)
</Frame>

1. Select Yes to Remove the Casual from the Job
