title: "Staff Summary"
---

The staff summary report exports details on every staff member that is registered with the organisation or casual worker that worked for the organisation in the selected time period. You can select either to **Export Full Details of Staff** or not. Exporting without just gives counts of the staff while full details gives all the available information of those staff members.
