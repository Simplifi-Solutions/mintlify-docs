---
title: "How to Schedule a Report"
---

To schedule an automated report:

1. Press Scheduled Reports under the Reports tab on the tool bar

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 16 13 12](/images/2026-03-30_16-13-12.png)
</Frame>

2. Fill out the scheduled reports form. You need to at least enter a name but also should update the schedule for when you'd like the report to run.

<Frame>
  ![2026 02 24 14 05 22](/images/2026-02-24_14-05-22.png)
</Frame>

3. Select what reports are included using **Add Report**
