title: "Published Jobs"
---

The **published jobs** report shows organisations and figures regarding jobs they have published.

**Example:**

| Organisation      | State      | Category | Jobs Published | Notifications Sent | Timesheets Created | Number Active Tempstaff | Head Office |
| ----------------- | ---------- | -------- | -------------- | ------------------ | ------------------ | ----------------------- | ----------- |
| Example Preschool | Wellington | ECE      | 20             | 68                 | 19                 | 87                      |             |

**Column Notes:**

- Head Office: Whether this organisation is a head office or not
