---
title: "Casual Jobs"
---

The casual jobs report is a report on the casuals registered with this organisation with their details and how many jobs they were notified of, accepted, declined and were assigned to within the selected time period.

**Example:**

| ID      | Name      | City         | State/Region | Current Active Organisations | Notifications | Accepted | Declined | Assigned |
| ------- | --------- | ------------ | ------------ | ---------------------------- | ------------- | -------- | -------- | -------- |
| ID39482 | Anna Mark | Christchurch | Canterbury   | 3                            | 4             | 11       | 2        | 3        |
