---
title: "How to Delete a Shift from the Roster"
---

<Frame>
  ![Screen Shot 2022 11 17 At 10 46 05 PM 1024x472](/images/Screen-Shot-2022-11-17-at-10.46.05-PM-1024x472.png)
</Frame>

1. Select the **Rostering** button.
2. Select the Department.
3. Select the **Daily** view.
4. Click on the shift you wish to delete.
5. Click on the bin icon to delete the shift.
6. Click on **Notify Changes** – StaffSync will notify the staff who have changes via push notifications on their phones.
