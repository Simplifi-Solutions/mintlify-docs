---
title: "Events Overview"
---

Whether you’re organising a small gathering or a large-scale event, effective staffing and space management are crucial to success.\
\
Simplifi’s Events module is designed to handle these complexities with ease, providing a comprehensive toolkit that adapts to your event’s unique needs.

- **Unified Rostering**

  Coordinate both space and staffing requirements by integrating areas like kitchens, event halls, or stages into your rosters, streamlining planning and execution.
- **Flexible Staff Allocation**

  Easily assign permanent employees or notify casual workers to fill roles based on availability and skills, ensuring every position is covered.
- **Dynamic Staffing Ratios**

  Use metrics such as guest counts or customer-to-staff ratios to determine optimal staffing levels, allowing you to scale effortlessly as event sizes fluctuate.
- **Real-Time Attendance Tracking**

  Monitor clock-in and clock-out times during events to capture attendance data accurately, ensuring seamless timesheet generation.
- **Automated Timesheets**

  Generate and manage detailed timesheets for approval by supervisors, staff, or even external parties like clients, reducing administrative workload.
