---
title: "How to Clock In/Out on Behalf of a Staff Member"
---

<Frame>
  ![Screen Shot 2022 11 24 At 10 38 31 PM 1024x626](/images/Screen-Shot-2022-11-24-at-10.38.31-PM-1024x626.png)
</Frame>

1. Select the **Schedule** button.

2. Click on the clock icon.

<Frame>
  ![Screen Shot 2022 11 24 At 10 39 52 PM](/images/Screen-Shot-2022-11-24-at-10.39.52-PM.png)
</Frame>

3. Switch the **Override?** toggle to **Yes**.

4. Enter the time.

5. Select **Update Timesheet Details**.
