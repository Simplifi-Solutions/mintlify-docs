title: "Accessing the Web App/Shadowing Users"
---

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 15 44 17](/images/2026-03-30_15-44-17.png)
</Frame>

To access the web app, go to the website of the system you'd like to access, Simplifi/Staffsync is either [apps.staffsync.com.nz](http://apps.staffsync.com.nz) or [apps.staffsync.com.au](http://apps.staffsync.com.au) and OnTask is [ontask.simplifi.work](http://ontask.simplifi.work).

If you enter your username and password, provided you are an admin, you will be greeted with the shadowing menu.

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 15 47 30](/images/2026-03-30_15-47-30.png)
</Frame>

You can search for and click on any user that pops up to 'shadow' their account, which lets you effectively log in as them. **MAKE SURE** that if you are changing settings that you are on the test server, otherwise the user you are shadowing will have their settings changed!

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 15 50 15](/images/2026-03-30_15-50-15.png)
</Frame>

Servers other than live will denote that they aren't live, and will have a different coloured banner at the top. Before you change anything, it is good practice to glance up here to make sure you really are on the test server!
