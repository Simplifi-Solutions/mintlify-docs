---
title: "Organisation Anniversary"
---

The organisation anniversary report shows organisations that have their anniversary of going live with StaffSync/Simplifi within the selected time date.

**Example:**

| Organisation Name | Payroll ID | Category | Anniversary Date | Head Office | Active Centres | Inactive Centres | User Count | Tempstaff Count | BDM       |
| ----------------- | ---------- | -------- | ---------------- | ----------- | -------------- | ---------------- | ---------- | --------------- | --------- |
| Test school       | 2131       | Schools  | 2-Mar-23         | No          | 1              | 0                | 4          | 6               | test.test |

**Column Notes:**

- **BDM**: This is the Business Development Manager. This is the primary contact in Simplifi for the organisation. This feature was archived in the March release.
