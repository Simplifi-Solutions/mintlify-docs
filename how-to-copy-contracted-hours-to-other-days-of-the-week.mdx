---
title: "How to Copy Contracted Hours to Other Days of the Week"
---

<Frame>
  ![Image 22 1024x844](/images/image-22-1024x844.png)
</Frame>

1. Select **Staff**
2. **Search** for the employee
3. Select the **Employee**
4. Select **Rostering Details**
5. Select the **Copy Icon** next to the day you wish to copy

<Frame>
  ![Image 23(1)](/images/image-23\(1\).webp)
</Frame>

1. Select **Copy** next to the hours you wish to copy from that day
2. Choose the days you wish to copy these hours onto
3. Select **Copy Hours**

<Frame>
  ![Image 24 1024x889](/images/image-24-1024x889.png)
</Frame>

1. Select **Reapply Contracted Hours**
2. Select **Save/Update Details**

A. You will see the copied hours in the ‘New Hours’ section of the selected days
