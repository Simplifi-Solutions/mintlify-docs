title: "Organisation Churn"
---

Organisation Churn gives you a report on the amount of organisations in different industries that have joined and left.

**Example:**

| Industry  | Clients Joined | Clients Left | Net Gain | Active at Period End | Clients Joined (Same Period Year Previous) | Clients Left (Same Period Year Previous) | Net Gain (Same Period Year Previous) | Active at Period End Year Previous | YoY Same Period Change (%) |
| --------- | -------------- | ------------ | -------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Education | 5              | 4            | 1        | 887                  | 2                                          | 0                                        | 0                                    | 1022                               | 100                        |
