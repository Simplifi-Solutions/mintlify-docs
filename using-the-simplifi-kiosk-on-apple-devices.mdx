**Using the Simplifi KIOSK on Apple Devices**

The Simplifi KIOSK is currently only available on the Google Play Store. However, the following workaround allows Apple device users to access it:

Open **Safari** and go to:\
https://apps.simplifi.work/?kiosk

1. Tap the **Share** icon (the square with an arrow pointing up).
2. Select **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Enter the name: **Simplifi KIOSK**.
4. Tap **Add** or **Done**.
5. You’ll now see a shortcut to the KIOSK on your Home Screen, and it will function much like an app.
