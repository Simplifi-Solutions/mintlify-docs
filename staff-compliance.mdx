Staff compliance information denotes which staff are complying with the compliance questions you have set up. If you have set up [Staff Information Collected](/job-information-fields) then you will see the compliance details.

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 12 44 56](/images/2026-03-30_12-44-56.png)
</Frame>

The staff will enter their own details, and it is up to you to verify it or not by pressing **verify** on the right.

You can also press **Send Message** to send a message to the staff member if they are missing compliance details.
