---
title: "How to Make a New Job Role"
---

Job roles are the titles or 'roles' within an organisation such as an "assistant manager" or "packer".

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 03 14 30 08](/images/2026-03-03_14-30-08.png)
</Frame>

To make a new role, press **Our Roles** from the **home** module. Then press **New Role** to start making a new role.
