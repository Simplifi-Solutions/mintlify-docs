---

To update your compliance:

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 12 55 55 1](/images/2026-03-30_12-55-55-1.png)
</Frame>

1. Press **Compliance**
2. Press the compliance registration requirement

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 30 13 08 39 1](/images/2026-03-30_13-08-39-1.png)
</Frame>

3. Enter the registration requirement details
4. Press **Save My Details**
