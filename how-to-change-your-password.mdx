---

1. Press **File** on the toolbar
2. Press **Change Password**

<Frame>
  ![2026 02 24 13 54 54](/images/2026-02-24_13-54-54.png)
</Frame>

3. Enter your old password and your new password twice

If you have forgotten your password, you will need to speak to either **Stephen** or **Pete** to get it reset.
