---
title: "Organisation Summary"
---

This report can only be made for every organisation in a portal. It cannot be made for a single organisation. Trying to make a report for a single organisation will result in an **error**.

**Example:**

| Organisation Name      | Payroll ID | Category | Region/State | Number Staff/Temps | Number Groups | Last Logon | Jobs Posted | Jobs Posted (Last 6 Months) | Job Days (Last 6 Months) | Jobs Filled (Last 6 Months) | Last Job Posted | Role Count |
| ---------------------- | ---------- | -------- | ------------ | ------------------ | ------------- | ---------- | ----------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------ | --------------------------- | --------------- | ---------- |
| Example Primary School | 4134       | Schools  | Wellington   | 34                 | 4             | 12/02/2024 | 15          | 2                           | 78%                      | 28/11/2024                  | 29/11/2024      | 20         |
