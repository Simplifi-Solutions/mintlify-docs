---
title: "How to Monitor Beacon Battery Levels"
---

<Frame>
  ![Beacon 2 1024x451](/images/beacon-2-1024x451.png)
</Frame>

1. Select **Our Profile**.
2. Select **Beacons**.
3. Select **Battery Monitor**.
4. Here, you will find an approximation of the battery level. We recommend replacing the battery once the level drops below 10%.
