---
title: "The Difference between a User and Staff"
---

**Users** are accounts that can administer the organisation and **Staff** are accounts for employees that are just for the organisation and being rostered/sent jobs etc.

You can set it so that specific user accounts can do staff roles such as requesting leave or accepting jobs by promoting or demoting users and vice versa. The user account is just an account that can access the admin side of Simplifi.
