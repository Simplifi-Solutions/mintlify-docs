---
title: "EOM Timesheets"
---

End of Month (EOM) Timesheets report is on the number of payroll timesheets processed by the end of the pay cycle month — split into the number of timesheets for casual staff and rostered staff.

**Example:**

| Organisations  | Category | Rostered | Casual Jobs |
| -------------- | -------- | -------- | ----------- |
| Example School | Schools  | 105      | 22          |
