---
title: "Viewing Audit Trails"
---

Audit trails contain information on different actions taken on an organisation, staff member or user which is critical for diagnosing issues.

To view the audit trails for an **organisation**, first search for one, click on it and press **Organisation Options** at the bottom.

<Frame>

The audit trails will be visible on the first option that opens.

To view the audit trails for a **staff member**, press the fourth tab after searching and selecting a staff member.
