One click breaks are an easy way for you to add meal and rest break times to the roster of your staff.

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 09 16 48 31](/images/2026-03-09_16-48-31.png)
</Frame>

To apply a one-click break, press **Start One-Click REST Breaks or MEAL Breaks**. 

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 09 16 50 26](/images/2026-03-09_16-50-26.png)
</Frame>

You will enter One-Click Break mode and will be able to select a time during the shift(s) to apply a break with the orange box. Press **Stop One-Click Breaks** once you have finished.
