title: "How to Unapprove a Timesheet"
---

<Frame>
  ![Screen Shot 2022 11 25 At 12 03 31 AM 1024x402](/images/Screen-Shot-2022-11-25-at-12.03.31-AM-1024x402.png)

1. Select the **Timesheets** button.

2. Switch this toggle to **Yes** to view the approved timesheets.

3. Select the date.

4. Select the timesheet you wish to view.

5. Select **Reverse Approval**.
