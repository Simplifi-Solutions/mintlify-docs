## Overview

Simplifi is a workforce management software made to make your work-load lighter, easier and more efficient.\
\
You can save time, cut admin and stay compliant with tools for staff scheduling, employee rostering, timesheet software and payroll software.

You can find help articles for employees [here](/employee-self-service).

You can find help articles for admins [here](/how-to-add-a-second-location).

There is an AI assistant that can be accessed next to the search bar that makes it very easy for you to find the article that you are looking for.
