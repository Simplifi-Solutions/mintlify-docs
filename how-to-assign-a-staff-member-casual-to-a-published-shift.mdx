1. Select the **Rostering** button.
2. Select the Department.
3. Select the **Weekly** view.
4. You will see an asterisk where you have published a job – click on it.

A. A summary of the published job is displayed here.

B. The responses to your job are displayed here.

5. To assign casuals to the shift, click on the green +.
6. Click **Assign to Job**.

C. Click here to view the list of casuals who have not responded. You can assign a casual from this list, however, please ensure you have verbal confirmation from the casual before you do so.

<Note>
  Note: The asterisk will disappear once you have filled the job
</Note>
