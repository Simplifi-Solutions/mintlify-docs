### About

Temp Staff are staff that aren't employed fully by the organisation, but work for the organisation on a need by need basis. You can open and modify individual temp staff in the tabs on the right of the screen.

There are 7 tabs you can edit through:

**Registration Option** allows you to modify answers to questions the temp staff have been asked and change their self-selected roles

**Entities Registered With** allows you to view and delete the relationships that the temp staff has with different organisations on Simplifi

**Job Notifications** allows you to view the notifications that they are getting, and whether they are receiving them or not

**Support Call Log/Audit Trail** allows you to see support calls this staff member has made and the audit trail allows you to see what actions they have taken

**Misc/Devices** allows you to see the web-sessions for this user and [**Manage Devices**](/manage-devices)

**Jobs/Timesheets** allows you to see the timesheets that have been generated for this staff member and the jobs they accepted

**Merge** allows you to merge two staff together if there is a double up in the system. Select the organisation on the left, then the doubled-up person on the right and press merge data to complete the merge
