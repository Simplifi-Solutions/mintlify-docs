You can troubleshoot a user's device using the manage devices window.

To get to this page, **Search** for the staff member you are looking for, select them and then select **Misc/Devices** from the right-hand side tabs.

Select a device that the staff member has registered on the left-hand side, and details will pop up for the device. If you wish to view details that OneSignal, our notification service, has on the device, press **Get OneSignal Device Details**.

You can also test notifications to that device on the far right-hand side.
