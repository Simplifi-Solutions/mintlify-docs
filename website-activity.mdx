---
title: "Website Activity"
---

The website activity report contains numbers for the amount of hits (visits) the specified portal gets and the amount of job days posted per hour.

**Example:**

| Date       | Hour | Hits   | Job Days Posted | Notifications Sent | Day | Jobs Published |
| ---------- | ---- | ------ | --------------- | ------------------ | --- | -------------- |
| 23/02/2026 | 3:00 | 413403 | 2               | Monday             | 1   |                |
