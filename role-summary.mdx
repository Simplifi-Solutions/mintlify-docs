---
title: "Role Summary"
---

The role summary report contains a list of roles as well as a count of users/workers in the role and jobs of this role that have been posted, filled and broadcast within the time frame selected.

**Example:**

| Role Name         | Total | Users Joined | Users Left | Jobs Posted | Jobs Filled | Jobs Published by Broadcast | Jobs Filled by Broadcast |
| ----------------- | ----- | ------------ | ---------- | ----------- | ----------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Teacher - Primary | 6713  | 17           | 63         | 1244        | 1199        | 384                         | 320                      |
